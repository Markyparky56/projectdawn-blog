About Me

Hey! I'm Mark, a student studying Computer Games Technology at Abertay University in Dundee, Scotland

I intend to use this site to document my ramblings and findings and to collect a portfolio of my work. This year I intend to attempt the OneGameAMonth "Jam", my progress which will be documented here, which is one of the reasons I setup this website.

I have an interest in graphics programming and procedural generation. I aspire to eventually create an release a freely available engine capable of producing polygon landscapes produced from voxel maps.
